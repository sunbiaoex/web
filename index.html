About me

{% include image.html url="/images/sunbiao.jpeg" caption="Me at Hainan in 2013" width=300 align="right" %}

In July 2013, I started as an assistant professor in the School of Electrical and Information Engineering, at Tianjin University (TJU).

I'm also a visiting research fellow with Department of Ophthalmology, National University Hospital (NUH), Singapore.

Before joining TJU, I received the Ph.D. degree in Electronics Science and Technology (Signal Processing) from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2013, under the supervision of Prof. Jianjun Jiang.
